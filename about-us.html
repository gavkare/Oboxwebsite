Work Process

1

Planning

Set a budget: Establish a realistic budget for the project, taking into account design fees, furniture and materials costs, contractors, and any additional expenses. Ensure that your budget aligns with your goals and expectations.

2

Design

Understand the client's needs: Begin by having a thorough discussion with the client to understand their requirements, preferences, and lifestyle.

3

Development

Detailed space planning: Refine the initial layout and floor plan based on the client's feedback and requirements. Ensure that the space is optimized for functionality, traffic flow, and spatial relationships. Consider zoning different areas for specific activities.

4

Presentation

Choose a suitable location for the presentation meeting. Ensure that the space is comfortable, well-lit, and free from distractions. If an in-person meeting is not possible, consider using video conferencing tools to present your design remotely.
